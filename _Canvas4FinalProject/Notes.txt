Know the width of text using CANVAS: "measureText(txt)". Example:
		var c=document.getElementById("myCanvas");
		var ctx=c.getContext("2d");
		ctx.font="30px Arial";
		var txt="Hello World"
		ctx.fillText("width:" + ctx.measureText(txt).width,10,50)
		ctx.fillText(txt,10,100);
